Resto-Reso (“Resto-Reso,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), operated by [Legal Business Name] in [City, Province — e.g. Toronto, Ontario], Canada, provides AI phone- and web-based reservation and order-taking services to restaurants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information through our website (restoreso.ca) and our services (the “Services”).

We handle personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy laws.

1. Who this policy covers

  • Website visitors — people who browse restoreso.ca or submit our contact form.
  • Client restaurants — businesses that sign up for the Services.
  • Diners & callers — people who call, book, or order through a restaurant that uses Resto-Reso. For this information we generally act as a service provider (processor) on behalf of the restaurant, which remains responsible for that data as the controller.

2. Information we collect

From website visitors

  • Details you submit in our contact/demo form: name, restaurant name, email, phone number, and any message or details about your current tools.
  • Basic technical data your browser sends automatically (e.g. IP address, device/browser type), and standard server logs.

From client restaurants

  • Account and contact details, restaurant configuration (hours, menu, tables, policies), and billing information processed by our payment provider.

From diners & callers (on behalf of restaurants)

  • Reservation and order details, name, and phone number.
  • Call audio and/or transcripts created when the AI agent answers a call, and messages sent through the website chatbot or online ordering page.

We do not collect payment card numbers for diners’ orders. Orders placed through the website ordering page are pay-at-pickup unless stated otherwise.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule demos.
  • To provide and operate the Services — answering calls, booking and managing reservations, taking orders, and showing them on the restaurant's dashboard.
  • To send transactional text messages (e.g. an order or booking confirmation) to the relevant diner, and alerts to the restaurant's manager.
  • To bill client restaurants and maintain our business records.
  • To maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the Services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4. Text messages (SMS)

When a diner provides a mobile number to make a booking or place an order, they may receive a transactional confirmation text. These are service messages related to the specific request — not marketing. Message and data rates may apply.

5. Service providers we share information with

We share personal information only as needed to run the Services, with providers bound to protect it. Our current providers include:

  • Formspree — processes website contact-form submissions.
  • Retell AI — powers the AI phone agent (call handling, transcription).
  • ElevenLabs — generates the agent's voice.
  • Twilio — sends SMS confirmations and alerts.
  • Google — demo scheduling and fonts served on this site.
  • [Hosting/infrastructure provider, e.g. Railway] — hosts the application and database.
  • [Payment processor, if any] — processes subscription billing.

We do not sell personal information. Some providers may process or store data outside Canada (e.g. in the United States); confirm and disclose this.

6. Retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. Reservation, order, and call records are retained for [retention period — e.g. 24 months], or as directed by the client restaurant, after which they are deleted or anonymized.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw consent. Because much diner data is held on behalf of a restaurant, we may direct your request to that restaurant or act on its instructions. To make a request, contact us at info@restoreso.ca.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information, including encryption in transit. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Cookies & analytics

This website does not use advertising cookies. If you add analytics (e.g. Google Analytics) or other cookies, disclose them here. Fonts are loaded from Google, which may receive your IP address as part of serving them.

10. Children

The Services are intended for restaurants and their adult customers and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their personal information.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
