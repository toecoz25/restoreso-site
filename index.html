    FAQ

    Questions, Answered

    How direct pickup works, what you're billed for, and how Resto-Reso fits with the tools you already use.

    Do I need to stop using delivery apps?
    No. Keep delivery apps where they provide value for delivery and customer discovery. Resto-Reso gives repeat and pickup customers a direct way to order by phone or online.
    Can it take delivery orders too?
    Yes. If you have your own drivers, the AI phone assistant can take delivery orders alongside pickup — you just handle the drop-off. For third-party delivery logistics, keep the delivery apps.
    What happens if the AI can't handle a call?
    It escalates to a human instead of guessing. Unusual requests or anything outside what it's been set up to handle get passed on, managers are alerted when something needs attention, and every call is logged in your dashboard. Calls that don't result in an order or booking are never billed.
    What if an order comes through wrong?
    Every completed phone order lands in your dashboard the moment it's captured, and the customer receives an SMS confirmation of what was ordered — so both sides can spot an issue before the kitchen starts. Your team always sees the full order details, and you're only billed for completed orders.
    How do customers find my direct pickup page?
    Restaurants can link their Resto-Reso ordering page from their website, Google Business Profile, social pages, QR codes, menus, receipts and takeout-bag inserts.
    Can Resto-Reso be used as my Google pickup-order link?
    Yes. During setup, we can help you prepare the ordering link and provide guidance for adding it to your Google Business Profile as a direct pickup option. We don't make external Google account changes without your authorization.
    Do I need separate phone and online pickup menus?
    No. Your AI phone orders and direct online orders can use the same pickup menu, pricing and item availability.
    Can I offer regular pickup prices?
    Yes. Because direct Resto-Reso orders are not charged a percentage of the order total, restaurants can use their regular pickup pricing rather than increasing direct pickup prices to absorb percentage fees.
    What counts as a completed phone order?
    A completed phone order is an order successfully captured by the AI phone assistant and submitted to the restaurant. General questions, abandoned calls, spam, wrong numbers and calls that do not result in an order are not billed as completed orders.
    Are direct online orders charged per order?
    No. Direct online pickup and delivery orders are included at no additional Resto-Reso order fee with Starter, Growth and Complete.
    How do customers pay?
    Customers pay the restaurant when they pick up their order or when the order is delivered. Resto-Reso does not currently process online customer payments.
    What happens above 300 completed phone orders?
    Restaurants receiving more than 300 completed phone orders per month receive custom high-volume pricing.
    What does setup include?
    The one-time $150 setup includes menu configuration, restaurant rules, AI phone assistant setup, call-flow testing, dashboard setup, staff onboarding and direct-pickup launch materials.
