The Problem

Restaurants Lose Revenue Every Day —
On Missed Calls and Commissions

Your staff are slammed serving guests. Calls go to voicemail, takeout orders bleed app commissions, and the business walks to a competitor.

📵

Calls go unanswered during peak hours

Staff can't serve tables and answer phones at once. Guests booking a table — or calling in a takeout order — hang up and try a competitor.

🌙

No one available after closing

Reservations and orders come in around the clock. Without 24/7 coverage, every after-hours booking and order disappears.

📉

You pay commission even on pickup

Delivery apps take 7–15% on pickup orders they don't even deliver — money on takeout you could simply keep.

💸

Every missed call is lost revenue

A table turns $200–$600 a seating, and every takeout order adds up. Missing even a few calls a week is thousands gone each month.
