The Problem

Restaurants Lose Revenue Every Day —
On Missed Calls and Marketplace Fees

Your staff are slammed serving guests. Calls go to voicemail, direct orders lose margin to marketplace fees, and the business walks to a competitor.

📵

Calls go unanswered during peak hours

Staff can't serve tables and answer phones at once. Guests booking a table — or calling in a takeout order — hang up and try a competitor.

🌙

No one available after closing

Reservations and orders come in around the clock. Without 24/7 coverage, every after-hours booking and order disappears.

📉

Percentage fees reduce the value of every pickup order

Marketplace apps can charge a percentage even when the customer picks up the food. Direct phone and online ordering gives repeat customers another way to order while helping the restaurant keep more of each sale.

💸

Every missed call is lost revenue

A table turns $200–$600 a seating, and every takeout order adds up. Missing even a few calls a week is thousands gone each month.

FAQ

Questions, Answered

The details behind the pricing, the plans and how Resto-Reso fits with what you already use.

What counts as a completed phone order?
A completed phone order is an order successfully captured by the AI receptionist and submitted to the restaurant. General questions, abandoned calls, spam, wrong numbers and calls that do not result in an order are not billed as completed orders.
Are online orders charged per order?
No. Online pickup and delivery orders are included at no additional order fee with Starter, Growth and Complete.
How do customers pay for online orders?
Customers pay the restaurant when they pick up the order or when the order is delivered. Resto-Reso does not currently process online customer payments.
Do I need to cancel Uber Eats or DoorDash?
No. Resto-Reso can work alongside marketplace apps. Restaurants can keep those services for discovery or delivery while giving existing and repeat customers a lower-cost way to order directly.
What is included in the setup fee?
The one-time $150 setup fee includes menu configuration, restaurant hours, pickup and delivery rules, reservation policies, seating configuration, AI receptionist setup, testing, dashboard setup and staff onboarding.
What happens if I receive more than 300 completed phone orders?
Restaurants receiving more than 300 completed phone orders per month receive custom high-volume pricing.
Are prices in Canadian dollars?
Yes. All displayed prices are in Canadian dollars and apply per restaurant location.
